# UK Legal Pack

## Silent Help - Regulatory Compliance Documentation

This folder contains the essential legal and regulatory documentation required for deploying a mental health application in the UK market.

---

## Contents

### 1. [DPIA-Template.md](./DPIA-Template.md)
**Data Protection Impact Assessment**

Required under UK GDPR Article 35 for processing that:
- Involves special category data (health data)
- May affect vulnerable individuals
- Uses automated decision-making

This DPIA covers:
- Data processing activities
- Risk assessment and mitigation
- Technical and organisational measures
- Data subject rights implementation
- Third-party processor management

### 2. [Hazard-Log-Template.md](./Hazard-Log-Template.md)
**Clinical Safety Hazard Register**

Required for compliance with:
- DCB0129: Clinical Risk Management for Health IT
- DCB0160: Deployment Clinical Risk Management
- MHRA Software as a Medical Device guidance

This Hazard Log covers:
- Risk classification matrix
- Identified clinical hazards
- Mitigation strategies
- Residual risk assessment
- Incident logging template

## Regulatory Framework

### UK GDPR & Data Protection Act 2018
- Special category data (health) processing
- Explicit consent requirements
- Data subject rights
- International data transfers

### UK Data Protection and Digital Information Act 2025
- Updated data processing requirements
- AI-specific provisions
- Enhanced enforcement powers

### DCB0129 / DCB0160
- Clinical safety case requirements
- Hazard identification and mitigation
- Safety throughout lifecycle

### MHRA Guidance
- Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) considerations
- AI/ML specific requirements
- Post-market surveillance
